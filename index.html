Katrina's Origami

Hi! I'm Katrina Mizuo, and I've been making origami since middle school. Although it's been one of my biggest passions, I've never actually gathered pictures of what I've made before. Through origami, I've connected with all kinds of people in my professional life, even going so far as to lead a teambuilding origami class in the office. Here have been some of my works over the years.

I first made origami anteaters to represent UCI. Since then, it's become a tradition to gift them to other UCI people I meet.

Anteater

When I wanted to make an origami spider but thought the tutorial looked too intimidating, I made an origami beetle instead.

Beetle

When looking for a way to use some green paper I had, origami cicadas came up (or maybe I just like making bugs).

Cicada

For the Lunar New Year, I made an origami dragon in 2024. It was yellow since I ran out of red paper.

Dragon

3D origami penguins made with modular pieces folded from many sheets of paper.

Penguin

Also for Lunar New Year, an origami rabbit made in 2023. The red paper is for good luck and prosperity in the coming year.

Rabbit
